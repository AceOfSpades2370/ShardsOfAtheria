Shards of Atheria is an ambitious project where we (I) aim to bring a cohesive story into Terraria, while also (hopefully) adding fun and interesting content (terraria mod #283637263). Currently it only adds content and there isn't much lore.

Join the Discord/Guilded server to report bugs, see spoilers and other things. Invite links in the Forum thread.
Visit the Forum thread for more info: https://forums.terraria.org/index.php?threads/shards-of-atheria-mod.112953/
